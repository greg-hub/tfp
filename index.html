The Frenulum Pendulum is a black comedy drama that follows a Northumberland farmer, Jackie, as he is forced to leave his struggling cow in labour to go help his best mate, Steve, who he finds naked in Donna’s bath tub with his broken penis under the showerhead.

The tale of debauchery deepens further as Jackie finds his other friend, Seppi, in the bed next door and Donna’s husband, a local police officer, is on his way home. Chaos ensues as Donna wants to keep the lads there to take the brunt of her husbands rage. Jackie faces the dilemma of whether to help his friends or leave them to get back to his cow.
